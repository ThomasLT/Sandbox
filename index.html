How can we Help?

Our role is to support ALL STAFF in the use of technology to support learning, teaching and productivity throughout the College. We can offer you and yourcolleagues training and support for using a wide range of technologies that the College currently offers. They include:

  • Mahara (new!) – e-Portfolio tool
  • Pro-Monitor – e-ILP
  • Moodle
  • Turnitin
  • Microsoft Office
  • MS Surface tablets
  • e-Learning Strategy
  • Collaborative learning technologies (web 2.0 e.g. Padlet, Popplet etc.)
  • Plus lots more...

The list is not exhaustive. Therefore, if you are looking for ideas of how you can usetechnology to support and engage learners, come and see us and we’ll investigate what technology is available to support your needs.
